You might have noticed that while doing some work you get completely submerged but in some you, are not enough focused and concentrated. This is where the flow state comes. In this article, you will read about what is flow state and how to achieve it. In addition, you will also read some other things related to flow state.

What is flow of state

Flow of state is the state when we are deeply focused on our work or the activity we are performing. The state of mind where no distractions can take our attention from our work to other thing or place. This type of state of mind is called flow state.

Flow state is the state of mind when we are completely immersed and absorbed while doing or performing an activity. If you get distracted by anything while doing your work, it means that you are not focused. You are not in a flow state. Flow state is the state when you forgets about the time and is completely submerged in your work. Your focus remains just on your work and not what is happening in your surroundings.

According to Wikipedia, in psychology flow state refers to the state of mind in which the person remains fully focused, enjoyed and energized.

Example of flow of state

Suppose that you are a person who loves to read books. You read books each day. In addition, if you are feeling energized, excited, focused and nothing can distract you from reading books each time then you are in flow state.

Flow state varies in each person, according to the person’s hobbies, skills and interest.

Benefits of flow state

There are a number of benefits of achieving a flow state. Obviously, if you are focused and super energetic in your work then the outputs of your work will be outstandable. Let’s see some of the benefits of flow state.

  • Motivation

    Flow state provides you motivation because when you will be in your flow state, you will get your most of the things done on time, which will provide you motivation. You will be motivated to do more and more things.

  • Positive-feelings

    Flow state fills you with positive feelings because you worked with full energy and productivity and also you are getting the results then you will be automatically filled with positive feelings. In addition, the positive feelings will encourage you to make yourself better each day in your work.

  • Concentration

    Flow of state increases your concentration on your work. You get absorbed in your work completely and thus it increases your concentration. Concentration is the most important thing while doing any work. If you are not concentrated in, your work then cannot you achieve success.

How long can a flow state last? -

A flow state can last from 25-30 minutes or even it can remain for hours. A person can achieve flow of state more than once in a day.

What does flow state feel like?

Whenever we are in flow state, we do all our work with 100% focus and concentration. In addition, if we are able to complete our work within time then obviously, we will feel pleasurable. Whenever, you will be in your flow state you will feel energized, engaged in your work completely. You will be enjoyed as well as focused during flow state. You can be eliminated from your stress and worries, whenever you are in flow state. In short, we feel ecstatic, enjoyed and focused during a flow state.

How much time does it take to get into flow state? -

It takes approximately 15-20 minutes to achieve flow of state, which can remain from 25-30 minutes to hours.

What is mindfulness?

Mindfulness means to do a task by taking your full time with concentration, in a very relaxed and easy way. Mindfulness is the opposite of rushing or being multitasking in a work. Examples of mindfulness activities can be meditation, deep breathing exercies etc.

Flow State V/S Mindfulness

Now, let us read what is the difference between flow state and mindfulness.

In simple terms, flow state refers to the state of mind when you are completely absorbed in work and forgets about everything like time, surrounding etc. The only thing you know that you have to do your work with full focus.

However, mindfulness is different from flow state, because in mindfulness state of mind you know what you are doing, you are not completely submerged in what you are doing. You know what is happening in and around your surroundings.

Therefore, this was the difference between mindfulness and flow state. It becomes difficult sometimes to distinguish between mindfulness and flow state. Because they are almost same but not completely. Mindfulness and flow state are the two different states of mind.

How to achieve flow of state?

  • Skills must match with the task:

    Your skills should match with the tasks you choose for yourself. So, that you can experience flow during your work. For example, a cricketer gets flow state easily because he/she knows how to play cricket or they have skills of playing cricket. Similarly, the tasks you are doing and you also have skills, then there are high chances of getting into flow state.

  • Set goals:

    Your goals must be clear before you perform any task. You must be aware of the reason of doing the task. Set goals for your tasks, so that it will remind you repeatedly about your goals and you will remain motivated and energized while doing your work. In addition, the chances increase of getting into flow state.

  • Stay in balance

    Stay in the balance of easy and difficult. Do not choose a task for yourself, which is too easy for you and the task, which is too difficult for you. Because if you will choose a very easy task for you then it will make you feel, boring and you will easily lose your interest. Similarly, if you will choose a very difficult task for you then it will make you will frustrated and there are high chances that you will give up your work. Try to choose the task, which is neither too easy nor too hard for you.

Summary

Stay in the balance of easy and difficult. Do not choose a task for yourself, which is too easy for you and the task, which is too difficult for you. Because if you will choose a very easy task for you then it will make you feel, boring and you will easily lose your interest. Similarly, if you will choose a very difficult task for you then it will make you will frustrated and there are high chances that you will give up your work. Try to choose the task, which is neither too easy nor too hard for you.
