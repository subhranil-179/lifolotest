What is meditation?

Meditation is the practice of clearing all the thoughts and stress for some time and just focusing on a particular thing. Just like you do physical exercises for your body, which helps you to remain healthy, active and energetic; your mind also requires exercises for better functioning, focus and concentration. Meditation is one of the most ancient mental exercises. The oldest form of meditation was discovered around 1500 BCE and still now you can see most of the people doing meditation, so as to remain more focused, calm, concentrated in their work and for being healthy in their day to day life.


You can also say or consider meditation as a skill which takes time to get mastered because forgetting everything and just concentrating on a particular thing isn’t that much easy task. You have to do meditation daily for mastering it and by reading this, you can get the idea that meditation has a number of benefits that is why it is still prevalent in now a day’s also and more people are eager to learn it.

Now, if you know what is the meaning of meditation is or what is meditation, let's know the benefits of meditation.

Benefits of meditation

  • Improves mental health

    Meditation improves your mental health, you can observe many changes after doing meditation, and you will feel very relaxed after doing meditation. It will make you mentally healthy and give you better results.

  • Improves focus and concentration

    Meditation helps in increasing focus and concentration. After doing meditation you will feel more focused and concentrated than before. You can be more focused and concentrated after doing meditation.

  • Improves energy

    Meditation also helps in increasing energy. Like you feel more energetic after doing physical exercises, mental exercise which is meditation helps in increasing energy and you can do all your work with more efficiency.

  • Reduces stress and anxiety

    If you feel that you are stressed and full of anxiety, then meditation is the best and natural treatment you can give to yourself. Meditation helps in reducing your stress and anxiety, if you do it correctly.

  • Helps to remain clam

    Meditation will help you in remaining calm in your everyday life. You can take better decisions and observe less anger in yourself. You can act very calmly and patiently in your day to day life.

    Therefore, by reading this now you are aware about the benefits of doing meditation. These are some of the major benefits of doing meditation. Now, let’s know about how to do meditation.

How to do meditation

If you are just a beginner and want to start meditation, then simply follow these steps:

  1. Step 1

    Select a place in your home or surrounding which is quiet and calm where no one will disturb you while meditating. Always select a noise free place where you can do meditation with full focus and concentration without any interruption.

  2. Step 2

    Sit according to the position in which you feel comfortable whether you sit by crossing your legs, sitting upon your knees etc. according to your level of comfort.

  3. Step 3

    Sit according to the position in which you feel comfortable whether you sit by crossing your legs, sitting upon your knees etc. according to your level of comfort.

  4. Step 4

    Try to keep your 100% focus and concentration in meditation not on your daily life stress and anxieties. When you are a beginner and just started doing meditation you will observe your mind going in different directions but don’t worry you will get rid of this problem with time and practice. Whenever you will feel your mind going in different directions try to get back at your present moment i.e. meditation and concentrate on your breathe again.

    Time – Select the time according to you, you can even start with few minutes i.e. 5 minutes of meditation to 10 minutes and then later on increase it with practice.

  5. Step 5

    Now simply open your eyes and relax for few minutes and then slowly get up. Do not get back to work just after doing meditation, relax for few minutes and then get back to work.
